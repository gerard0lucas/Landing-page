BCA Program Overview at Koghys Group of Institutions

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) at Koghys Group of Institutions is a three-year undergraduate degree program for students who wish to delve into the realm of computer languages and software development. One of the most sought-after courses in the field of Information Technology, our BCA program is tailored to meet the growing demand for well-qualified and skilled professionals in the tech industry.

Course Duration: 6 Semesters (3 Years).

Eligibility Criteria: Candidates should have passed 2nd PUC / 12th std.

What You Will Learn:

Foundational Knowledge:
Gain a solid foundation in core computer science concepts, mathematics, and the principles of programming languages.

Programming Skills:
Master various programming languages such as C, C++, Java, Python, and HTML, enabling you to develop and troubleshoot software effectively.

Database Management:
Learn about database management systems, data structures, and algorithms, equipping you with the skills to organize, store, and manage data efficiently.

Web Development:
Dive into web design and development, learning about web technologies, user interface design, and user experience principles.

Networking and Security:
Get introduced to the basics of networking, security principles, and cybersecurity measures to protect data and manage network operations.

Software Engineering:
Understand the systematic approach to the development, operation, maintenance, and retirement of software.

Emerging Technologies:
Stay ahead with knowledge of emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Blockchain, and Cloud Computing, which are transforming the tech landscape.
